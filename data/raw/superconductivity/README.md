# Superconductivity - Primary Sources

This directory contains excerpts and notes from **original research papers and textbooks** on superconductivity.

## Core Papers

### BCS Theory (Foundational)
1. **Bardeen, J., Cooper, L. N., & Schrieffer, J. R. (1957).** "Theory of Superconductivity." *Physical Review*, 108(5), 1175.
   - DOI: [10.1103/PhysRev.108.1175](https://doi.org/10.1103/PhysRev.108.1175)
   - The original BCS paper proposing Cooper pair formation via phonon mediation

2. **Cooper, L. N. (1956).** "Bound Electron Pairs in a Degenerate Fermi Gas." *Physical Review*, 104(1), 35.
   - DOI: [10.1103/PhysRev.104.35](https://doi.org/10.1103/PhysRev.104.35)
   - Introduction of Cooper pairs

### Key Experimental Papers
3. **Bednorz, J. G., & Müller, K. A. (1986).** "Possible high Tc superconductivity in the Ba‐La‐Cu‐O system." *Zeitschrift für Physik B Condensed Matter*, 64(2), 189-193.
   - DOI: [10.1007/BF01303701](https://doi.org/10.1007/BF01303701)
   - Discovery of high-Tc superconductivity

### Review Articles
4. **Scalapino, D. J. (1969).** "The BCS Theory of Superconductivity." In *Superconductivity* (pp. 449-513). Marcel Dekker.
   - Comprehensive review of BCS theory

## Textbooks
5. **Tinkham, M. (2004).** *Introduction to Superconductivity* (2nd ed.). Dover Publications.
   - ISBN: 978-0486435039
   - Standard graduate-level textbook

6. **Schrieffer, J. R. (1999).** *Theory of Superconductivity*. Westview Press.
   - ISBN: 978-0738201368
   - Original theorist's perspective
