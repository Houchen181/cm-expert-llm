### Quantum Hall Effect
1. **von Klitzing, K., Dorda, G., & Pepper, M. (1980).** "New Method for High-Accuracy Determination of the Fine-Structure Constant Based on Quantized Hall Resistance." *Physical Review Letters*, 45(6), 494.
