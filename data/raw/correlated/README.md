# Correlated Electron Systems - Primary Sources

This directory contains excerpts and notes from **original research papers and textbooks** on strongly correlated electron systems.

## Core Papers

### Mott Insulators
1. **Mott, N. F. (1949).** "The Basis of the Electron Theory of Metals, with Special Reference to the Transition Metals." *Proceedings of the Physical Society. Section A*, 62(7), 416.
   - DOI: [10.1088/0370-1298/62/7/303](https://doi.org/10.1088/0370-1298/62/7/303)
   - Original Mott insulator concept

2. **Hubbard, J. (1963).** "Electron Correlations in Narrow Energy Bands." *Proceedings of the Royal Society of London. Series A*, 276(1365), 238-257.
   - DOI: [10.1098/rspa.1963.0204](https://doi.org/10.1098/rspa.1963.0204)
   - Hubbard model formulation

3. **Anderson, P. W. (1963).** "Antiferromagnetism. Theory of Superexchange Interaction." *Physical Review*, 115(1), 2.
   - DOI: [10.1103/PhysRev.115.2](https://doi.org/10.1103/PhysRev.115.2)
   - Superexchange in Mott systems

### High-Tc Superconductors
4. **Anderson, P. W. (1987).** "The Resonating Valence Bond State in La₂CuO₄ and Superconductivity." *Science*, 235(4792), 1196-1198.
   - DOI: [10.1126/science.235.4792.1196](https://doi.org/10.1126/science.235.4792.1196)
   - RVB theory of high-Tc superconductivity

5. **Zhang, F. C., & Rice, T. M. (1988).** "Effective Hamiltonian for the superconducting Cu oxides." *Physical Review B*, 37(7), 3759.
   - DOI: [10.1103/PhysRevB.37.3759](https://doi.org/10.1103/PhysRevB.37.3759)
   - t-J model derivation

### Heavy Fermions
6. **Stewart, G. R. (1984).** "Heavy-fermion systems." *Reviews of Modern Physics*, 56(4), 755.
   - DOI: [10.1103/RevModPhys.56.755](https://doi.org/10.1103/RevModPhys.56.755)
   - Comprehensive review

## Textbooks
7. **Imada, M., Fujimori, A., & Tokura, Y. (1998).** "Metal-insulator transitions." *Reviews of Modern Physics*, 70(4), 1039.
   - DOI: [10.1103/RevModPhys.70.1039](https://doi.org/10.1103/RevModPhys.70.1039)
   - Comprehensive review of Mott transitions

8. **Georges, A., Kotliar, G., Krauth, W., & Rozenberg, M. J. (1996).** "Dynamical mean-field theory of strongly correlated fermion systems and the limit of infinite dimensions." *Reviews of Modern Physics*, 68(1), 13.
   - DOI: [10.1103/RevModPhys.68.13](https://doi.org/10.1103/RevModPhys.68.13)
   - DMFT review
